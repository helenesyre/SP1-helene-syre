Visit

Come take a journey through time and discover how life on Earth has transformed over millions of years! From tiny single - celled organisms to giant dinosaurs - and eventually us-evolution tells the incredible story of how everything came to be.

Location

The museum is located at Communitu Science Museum, Kirsten Flagstads plass 1, 0150 Oslo.

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Food & drink

There is a caf attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Explore, Learn, Imagine!

Whether you're a curious kid, a dedicated teacher, or a passionate researcher, there's something for everyone to explore at our museum. Dive into a world of discovery tailored just for you!

Learn more

For Kids

Discover science in action with hands-on exhibits, meet ancient creatures like the Woolly Mammoth, and join clubs that spark your curiosity. Every visit is an adventure!

For Teachers

Bring your classroom to life with guided tours, interactive lessons in our learning lab, and exciting video presentations. We're here to support and inspire your students.

Researchers

Access our digital archives, collaborate in our lab spaces, and connect with academic partners across the country. Join our community of discovery and innovation.

Location

Community Science Museum, Kirsten Flagstads plass 1, 0150 Oslo.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
