Evolution

Come take a journey through time and discover how life on Earth has transformed over millions of years! From tiny single-celled organisms to giant dinosaurs - and eventually us - evolution tells the incredible story of how everything came to be.

Where it all began

Long before humans walked the Earth, life started in the oceans as microscopic organisms. Over time, these tiny forms of life evolved into the wide variety of plants, animals, and creatures we know today. Learn how natural selection helped shape every living thing, from the tiniest bug to the biggest whale.

Darwin’s Big Idea

In the 1800s, Charles Darwin introduced the theory of evolution by natural selection - a groundbreaking idea that changed the way we understand life forever. Find out what inspired him, how his ideas were received, and why they still matter today.

Creatures of the Past

Not all species made it to the present. Get up close with the animals that didn’t survive, including the towering dinosaurs, the unlucky Dodo, and our furry favorite—the Woolly Mammoth. These extinct creatures help us understand how life adapts… or doesn’t.

Humans in the Picture

Where do we fit into all of this? Trace the evolutionary journey from early primates to modern humans. See how our brains, bodies, and tools have changed, and explore what might be next for us in the story of life.
